G U R A D I A N S

Defeat the invading minions and protect your crystal!

How to play:

  • Drag guards to the battlefield to summon them

  • Summoning guard will reduce your available cost

  • Cost will regenerate over time

  • You lose a life a minion moves past your crystal

  • You lost when life decrease to zero

  • Defend until there is no minion on the battlefield. Have fun!

Class:

Skill:

Vanguard

Summon at low cost; tanky

Archer

Ranged attack; squishy

Berserker

Attack ALL blocking enemies

Priest

Heal allies; can't attack
